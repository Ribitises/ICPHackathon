Frequently Asked Questions
Are all nursing monitoring and documentation sheets included in SwiftChart?
Currently, users can add and view data on Monitoring Sheets, Fluid I&O Monitoring Record, TPR Sheet, and Nurses Notes(FDAR). The developers are prioritizing sheets used in various wards, and other sheets are being considered for future updates.
Who can see the patient data with SwiftChart?
Currently, users can add and view data on Monitoring Sheets, Fluid I&O Monitoring Record, TPR Sheet, and Nurses Notes(FDAR). The developers are prioritizing sheets used in various wards, and other sheets are being considered for future updates.To protect the patient data, SwiftCare made patient data limited to healthcare professionals who provide direct care to the patient.
Can everyone add patient data with SwiftChart?
Currently, only nurses and nurse assistants can add data since the available sheets pertain to their roles.
What wards are included in SwiftChart?
All patient wards are included in the system, but users will only see wards and patients assigned to them.
